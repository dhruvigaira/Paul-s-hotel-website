DELICIOUS DINING

For a serene escape, revel in the laid-back luxury of the hotel, where panoramic Maldivian
views accompany your refined international dining experience at Windows restaurant. Sip
on single malts and handcrafted cocktails in the vibrant ambiance of Raha, celebrated as
one of the Maldives' most popular nighttime venues. Whether you're dining, sipping, or just soaking
in the stunning surroundings, this Maldivian getaway promises a perfect blend of relaxation and indulgence.
